Welcome to My Random Facts Website

Discover fascinating and surprising facts

Fact 1

The shortest war in history was between Britain and Zanzibar in 1896. It lasted only 38 minutes!

Fact 2

Honey never spoils. Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible.

Fact 3

There is a species of jellyfish that can revert back to its earliest stage of life, essentially making it immortal.

Fact 4

The Great Wall of China is not visible from space with the naked eye, despite popular belief. Astronauts can only see it with the help of binoculars or a zoom lens.

Fact 5

Octopuses have three hearts. Two hearts pump blood to the gills, and the third heart circulates it to the rest of the body.
